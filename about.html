About IITC

Ingress Intel Total Conversion (IITC) is an add-on that works as an overlay for the official Ingress intel map. IITC completely changes the way you work with the map, significantly increases efficiency by applying hardware acceleration and expands the capabilities via the use of various plugins.

IITC has long become the basis of numerous tools for both factions, as it serves as a platform for different applications and plugins. Besides several confidential plugins, which help coordinate operations for a team of agents, there are also public ones, available to both factions.

Stefan Breunig was the main driving force.

IITC "Community Edition" (IITC-CE) started by modos189 to continue development of stalled iitc-project. Many the work here is by others. See the Github commit log for full details.
